Who We Are

PT Coco Reina Global Charcoal Indonesia (brand: Mulia Charcoal) is a B2B wholesale manufacturer and exporter of coconut shell charcoal briquettes, also known as shisha charcoal or hookah charcoal. The company was founded in 2018 and is based in Semarang, Central Java, Indonesia. We sell exclusively to wholesale buyers and importers; we do not sell at retail.

What We Offer

Coconut shell charcoal briquettes for shisha and hookah, produced in the following shapes and sizes:

  • Cube — 25mm and 26mm
  • Hexagonal — 22×50mm and 26×50mm
  • Finger
  • Dome
  • Flat

All briquettes are made from 100% coconut shell with no chemical additives. Both natural quick-light and slow-burn variants are available. Private label and white label production (custom inner box and master carton printing) is offered to qualifying buyers.

Minimum Order and Pricing

  • MOQ: one 20ft container, approximately 18 metric tons.
  • Incoterm: FOB Semarang (Tanjung Emas Port), Indonesia.
  • Pricing: quoted per metric ton in USD. Price varies by shape, size, and packaging specification.
  • No retail. No smaller quantities, no LCL, no parcel orders. Buyers must inquire directly for a quote.

How We Are Different

  • Single-origin coconut shell sourced from Java and Sumatra.
  • In-house production, not a trading agent or broker.
  • MSDS and COA (Certificate of Analysis) supplied with every batch.
  • Halal-friendly production process.
  • Export-ready with full shipping documentation: Bill of Lading, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, and Phytosanitary Certificate.

Quality and Certifications

  • ISO 9001:2015 quality management process (auditors: Carsurin, Backjorindo).
  • MSDS available for every shipment.
  • COA issued per batch.
  • Ash content: less than 3%.
  • Moisture content: less than 5%.
  • Burning time: more than 60 minutes for the standard 25mm cube.
  • Self-Heating Test (SHT) certificate and Preferential Certificate of Origin issued on request.

Logistics

  • Port of loading: Tanjung Emas Port, Semarang, Indonesia (UN/LOCODE IDSRG).
  • Incoterms accepted: FOB, CIF, CFR.
  • UN classification: IMDG Code UN 1361 Class 4.2 (spontaneous combustion); charcoal, non-activated.
  • Lead time: approximately 14–21 days from receipt of the 30% T/T deposit to ready-for-shipment.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is one 20ft container, approximately 18 metric tons. We do not sell retail or smaller quantities.

What is the production lead time?

Standard production lead time is 14 to 21 days from receipt of the 30 percent T/T deposit, before sea freight transit.

Do you offer private label or white label?

Yes. Private label and white label production is available, including custom inner box and master carton printing. Minimum order applies per SKU.

What shapes and sizes are available?

Cube 25mm and 26mm, hexagonal 22×50mm and 26×50mm, finger, dome, and flat. All shapes are produced from 100 percent coconut shell with no additives.

What are the payment terms?

30 percent T/T deposit on order confirmation, 70 percent balance against a copy of the bill of lading. L/C at sight is also accepted. All quotes are in USD, FOB Semarang.

Are samples available?

Yes. Paid samples are shipped on request after KYC. Sample fee and courier cost are quoted by destination. Request samples at https://muliacharcoal.com/samples.

What is the UN/IMDG classification of your charcoal?

Coconut shell charcoal briquettes ship under IMDG Code UN 1361 Class 4.2 (spontaneous combustion). Each shipment includes MSDS and a Self-Heating Test certificate.

Do you sell retail or to individual consumers?

No. We are a B2B factory and only accept wholesale orders of one 20ft container or more. Retail buyers should contact a local distributor in their country.

From which port do you ship?

All shipments load at Tanjung Emas Port, Semarang, Indonesia (UN/LOCODE IDSRG). Standard Incoterms are FOB, CIF, and CFR.
