## About

Mulia Charcoal is the trading name of PT Coco Reina Global Charcoal Indonesia,
an Indonesian direct manufacturer and wholesale exporter of 100% natural
coconut shell charcoal briquettes engineered for shisha and hookah use.

- Founded: 2015
- Brand domain: muliacharcoal.com
- Factory region: Semarang, Central Java, Indonesia
- Port of loading: Semarang (Tanjung Emas, IDSRG), FOB
- Production capacity: 14 tons per day (350 tons per month)
- Production lines: 4 (8 ovens, 1.5-ton batches, 24-hour cycle)
- Raw material: coconut shell sourced from Java and Sumatra
- Minimum order quantity: 18 tons (one 20-foot container)
- Production lead time: 14–21 days (14 repeat brand, 21 new brand)
- Payment terms: 50% T/T advance, 50% T/T three days before loading
- Currency quoted: USD
- OEM / private-label: available
- Sales languages: English, Arabic, Russian, German, Turkish
- Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 (audited by Carsurin and Beckjorindo)
- Maritime compliance: IMDG Code UN 1361 Class 4.2 (spontaneous combustion safety)
