## About

Mulia Charcoal is the trading name of PT Coco Reina Global Charcoal Indonesia,
an Indonesian direct manufacturer and wholesale exporter of 100% natural
coconut shell charcoal briquettes engineered for shisha and hookah use.

- Founded: 2018
- Factory location: Jl. Mayor Unus KM 1.5, Mertoyudan, Magelang 56172, Central Java, Indonesia
- Port of loading: FOB Semarang, Indonesia
- Production capacity: 12 tons per day
- Minimum order quantity: 18 tons (one 20-foot container)
- Production lead time: 14–21 days
- Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 (audited by Carsurin & Backjorindo laboratoires)
- Maritime compliance: IMDG Code UN 1361 Class 4.2 (spontaneous combustion safety)
